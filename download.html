What if I don't own a smartwatch?

We realize that a smartwatch is still an expensive luxury for many travelers. The Local will still work without a smartwatch. However, we want you to be able to keep your phone in your pocket or bag as much as possible, so we highly recommend you use a smartwatch.


To facilitate this we are working with cell phone providers around the world to allow you to rent a smartwatch when you purchase a roaming plan for your vacation. You can contact your provider for more details.
