FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

• My Smartwatch does not display anything when using the App.

Your phone and watch are likely out of sync. Go to Settings --> Re-Sync and it should work.

• There is very little data for the area I am visiting.

Sounds like your filters are limiting your data too much. Go to Settings --> Filters --> Remove Filters.

• I can't hear the translations the App produces.

Check the volume setting for your phone or watch.
