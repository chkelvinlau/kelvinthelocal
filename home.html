Introducing "The Local"
A travel companion app that gives you the benefits of a trusted local guide while traveling - all conveniently on your smartphone or wrist!
App Features

• Phrase Translator:

View the spoken and written forms of common and typical travel phrases and questions

• Itinerary Details:

Quick and easy-to-view access to all of your itinerary details

• Emergency Button:

Press and hold for immediate connection to emergency assistance

• Around You:

Details everything happening around you: including warnings, events, laws, and restricted areas

• Expert Information:

Recieve information from tourist boards and experieced travelers.
